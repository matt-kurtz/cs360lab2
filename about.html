About Me

Hello! I'm currently majoring in Computer Science, a field that I find both challenging and incredibly rewarding. From algorithms to software development, I enjoy the problem-solving aspect of the discipline and the endless possibilities it offers in shaping the future.

As I approach graduation, I'm contemplating my next steps for grad school. The decision on what degree to pursue is not an easy one. The diverse fields within Computer Science, such as artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and data science, make the choice exciting yet challenging.

My goal is to find a program that aligns with my passion and career aspirations. Whether it's delving deeper into machine learning, exploring the intersection of technology and business, or focusing on cybersecurity challenges, I'm excited about the possibilities that grad school can offer.

I look forward to the next chapter of my academic journey and the opportunities for growth and learning that it will bring!
